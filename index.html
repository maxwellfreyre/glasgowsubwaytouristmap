Glasgow Subway Tourist Map

Welcome to the Glasgow Subway Tourist Map

This is an interactive map of the Glasgow Subway with Points of Interest (POIs) as described in Online Source material from the Strathclyde Partnership for Transport.

  • Hover over stations or POIs to quickly view their names.
  • Click an orange subway station to see nearby POIs. Then, click a nearby POI to render navigation.
  • Click a Legend Category to highlight related POIs on the map. Press a highlighted POI to render navigation.
  • Click a POI independent of other functions to render its navigation from the nearest subway station.
  • Zoom, pan, or use your location to explore the map in detail. Press anywhere to undo an interaction.

    • Sources: OSM OS OpenMap-Local SPT Subway PDF
